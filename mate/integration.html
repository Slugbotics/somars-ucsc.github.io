Ongoing Projects

Integration always has a set of hands-on projects running in parallel. These are some of the core builds we're working on this season:

Build a Buck Converter

Design and assemble a high-current buck converter to step 48 V down to the ROV's 12 V/5 V rails. You'll work on layout, thermal considerations, and testing under load.

Build a Capacitor Bank

Develop a capacitor bank to handle transient current spikes and keep our power rails stable when thrusters and manipulators switch on. Includes sizing caps, safety, and mounting.

CAD the Enclosure Plates

CAD the internal plates and mounting hardware that live inside our electronics enclosure so boards, converters, and wiring are secure and serviceable.

Skills You'll Learn in Integration

Integration is a great place to build cross-discipline experience. You'll touch hardware, CAD, and debugging all in the same week.

  • PCB design and layout (power + signal)
  • Basic electro-physics: current, transients, filtering
  • Harness design and connector selection
  • CAD for internal plates, mounts, and enclosure layouts
  • Debugging tools: multimeters, scopes, bench supplies
  • Documentation and wiring diagrams for future seasons
