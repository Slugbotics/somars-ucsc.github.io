Subteam · 03

Perception.

Computer vision for autonomous navigation and real-time object detection from the air.

About

Giving the drone
its eyes.

The Perception subteam develops the computer vision pipelines that let the drone see the world below, identify competition targets, and feed spatial information back to the autonomy stack.

We work with onboard cameras, train detection models, and implement real-time inference pipelines that run reliably at altitude during fully autonomous flight.

📷 Camera feed / detection screenshot

Focus Areas

What we work on.

Target Detection & Classification

Training and deploying object detection models (YOLO-based) to locate and classify competition targets — shapes, letters, colors — from aerial imagery.

Geolocation

Computing GPS coordinates of detected targets by combining camera geometry, drone pose (GPS + attitude), and ground-plane projection.

Obstacle Avoidance Vision

Detecting and localizing aerial obstacles using camera feeds so the autonomy stack can plan safe paths.

Dataset Collection & Training

Building labeled datasets of aerial imagery and training detection models using simulation renders and real field data.
