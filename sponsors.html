Sponsorship

SOMARS is made possible through the generous support of our sponsors. Help us build the next generation of autonomous drones at UCSC.

Why Sponsor SOMARS?

Your support directly funds competition entry fees, drone hardware, electronics components, manufacturing materials, and travel to national competitions like SUAS and C-UASC.

In return, sponsors gain visibility with a technically talented student audience, recognition at competitions, and the opportunity to connect with students entering the workforce in aerospace, robotics, and software engineering.
